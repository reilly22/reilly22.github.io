The Fluff: A bit about me.

I’m from Chicago, Illinois and am currently a senior at Purdue University. I’ve gained experience in lean manufacturing, continuous improvement, and project management through internships at BraunAbility Inc. (2019) and Conagra Brands (2020).

At Purdue, I’m studying Industrial Engineering. My coursework has focused on business and data analytics, data visualization, and engineering applications in a business setting. My main interests lie somewhere at the intersection of business and data science, using both to make intelligent decisions to meet the short & long-term goals of a company.

When I’m not occupied with academics & work, I can be found singing in a choral ensemble, drinking too much coffee, roadtripping, or planning my next trip.
